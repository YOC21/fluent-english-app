The 3 Golden Rules

Foundation for rapid fluency

1

Think in English

Stop translating from your native language. Rewire your brain to think directly in English. Read signs in English, have conversations in your head in English.

2

Power of Immersion

Surround yourself with English completely. Watch English movies, listen to podcasts, read books, and speak English whenever possible.

3

Embrace Mistakes

Mistakes are learning opportunities. Every error teaches you how NOT to do something. Don't be afraid to speak!
